About Me

Get to know the person behind the lens

3+ Years Experience

Hi, I'm Dukes Yatani

I believe every moment deserves to be remembered, and every story deserves to be told through images that speak.

My journey into photography started 3 years ago when I picked up my first camera at a friend's wedding. That single moment changed everything. I realized that I had a gift for capturing not just what people look like, but how they feel.

Since then, I've photographed over 15 weddings, countless portraits, and corporate events across Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and beyond. Each shoot has taught me something new about light, emotion, and the art of making people feel comfortable in front of the camera.
